About me!

Penny Wong is an illustrator and character designer specialising in digital and traditional mediums.

A graduate of Game Design and Production at the University of Salford, England, Penny’s work focuses on evoking emotions and communication through a vibrant selection of colours to produce illustrative and character design work that stands out and shines. She specialises in digital mediums with expertise in Procreate, with a clear artistic direction for not only games, but also in other media such as animation and film. With a touch of her visually appealing cartoony artstyle, she can bring any project a dash of charm and vibrance. She’s also capable of adapting her artstyle to fit in different areas of projects. Currently, she works as a freelance independent illustrator and has experience working within a contemporary pipeline and complex projects within indie game studios. She’s a strong and capable team player, capable of working within deadlines. Her works have been featured in the Games Transformed 2023 game jam and commission work for clients.


You can find me on Tumblr, Twitter or even itch.io!
